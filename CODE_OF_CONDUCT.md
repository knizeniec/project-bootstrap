# Code of Conduct

This repository expects respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication and review feedback.
- Focus critique on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent while holding quality standards.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or abusive language.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration workflows.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or limit participation for behavior that violates this policy.
